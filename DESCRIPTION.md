# fmr.fyi - Fair Market Rent Data & Cash Flow Analysis

fmr.fyi provides instant access to Fair Market Rent (FMR) data from the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) and powerful cash flow analysis tools for real estate investors.

## Web Platform

Our web platform allows you to quickly search for FMR data by address, city, ZIP code, county, or state. We offer various search options, including popular searches, top markets by average FMR, and price jumps, to help you find the information you need.

## Chrome Extension

Our Chrome extension transforms how you evaluate rental properties by displaying instant cash flow calculations directly on property listings from Zillow and Redfin. More sites coming soon.

### Key Features:

- **Instant Cash Flow Badges** - See monthly cash flow estimates (positive/negative) directly on property listings
- **Smart Property Detection** - Automatically extracts address, price, bedrooms, and HOA fees from listings
- **Comprehensive Financial Analysis** - Calculates cash flow including mortgage, taxes, insurance, HOA, and property management costs
- **Official HUD Data** - Uses Fair Market Rent (FMR) and Small Area FMR (SAFMR) data from HUD
- **Detailed Analysis Modal** - Click any badge to see complete FMR data by bedroom count and customizable financial parameters
- **Works Everywhere** - Supports list views, map views, and detail pages across multiple real estate sites
- **Fully Customizable** - Adjust down payment, tax rates, mortgage rates, property management fees, and more
- **Smart Caching** - Fast performance with intelligent caching of calculations and data

Our goal is to make FMR data readily available and simple to understand, helping real estate investors, property managers, and professionals make smarter, data-driven decisions.
